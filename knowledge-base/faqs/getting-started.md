# Getting Started FAQ

## General Questions

### Q: How do I contact support?
**A:** You can reach support through our chatbot interface, which is available 24/7. For complex issues, the system will automatically escalate to our human support team.

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes. We use a privacy-first architecture:
- Personal information is never stored in public repositories
- All data is anonymized before analysis
- Private data is encrypted and stored separately

### Q: How fast are responses?
**A:** Typical response times:
- AI responses: Under 10 seconds
- Human escalation: Within 30 minutes during business hours
- Complex issues: Follow-up within 24 hours

### Q: Can I see system performance?
**A:** Yes! Visit our public dashboard for real-time metrics on:
- System status and uptime
- Response times and resolution rates
- Knowledge base growth
- Customer satisfaction trends

## Technical Questions

### Q: What AI powers the system?
**A:** We use Google's Gemini AI for natural language understanding and response generation.

### Q: How does the knowledge base grow?
**A:** The system automatically:
1. Identifies repeated questions
2. Creates new knowledge articles
3. Updates existing articles with better solutions
4. Removes outdated information

### Q: Can I contribute to the knowledge base?
**A:** Currently, the system is autonomous. However, public knowledge base improvements are tracked and can be reviewed on GitHub.
